The Begum Hazrat Mahal National Scholarship

Eligiblity:Girls student belonging to minority communities

Scholarship Amount:INR 10,000 to INR 12,000 annually

Last Date to apply:30 September 2023

The central government organizes the Begum Hazrat Mahal National scholarship under the Ministry of Minority Affairs and is available for girl students studying in classes 9 to 12. This scholarship program for class 10 aims to provide financial assistance to the students belonging to economically weaker sections of society. The selected students will be rewarded with an amount of INR 10,000 to INR 12,000 annually .

The DCE Scholarship

Eligiblity:The scholarship is open for students who belong to Minority community (Muslim, Christian, Parsi, Sikh, Buddhist and Jains). They must be pursuing an ITI course. The annual income of the family should be less than INR 8 Lakh per annum.

Scholarship Amount:upto 10,000

Last Date to apply:January 1st of every year

The DCE scholarship is provided by the Department of Collegiate Education (DCE) and is available for the students studying in class 10 to post-graduation degrees. This scholarship program aims to provide financial aid to deserving candidates belonging to the economically weaker sections of society. The family earnings of the candidates should not exceed INR 8,00,000 per annum.

Vidyadhan Scholarship

Eligiblity:Candidates should have passed the class X board exam.

Scholarship Amount:varies from Rs 10,000 to Rs 60,000 per year

The Vidyadhan scholarship is an initiative of the Sarojini Damodaran Foundation. This scholarship program for class 10 provides scholarship opportunities to students in class 10. Under this scholarship program, over 2800 students have availed the scholarship benefits. The selected students will get up to INR 60,000 per annum.
