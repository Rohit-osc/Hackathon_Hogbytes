SSO Scholarship

Eligiblity:Candidates from class XI to postgraduate degree
Scholarship Amount:Up to INR 25,000
Last Date to apply:July 31, 2023

The SSO scholarship is an initiative of the state government of Rajasthan. This scholarship scheme is available for the students studying in class 11 to post-graduation courses. The main purpose behind this scholarship program is to provide financial aid to underprivileged meritorious students who are domiciles of Rajasthan. Under this scholarship program, the candidates can benefit from various post-matric schemes by Rajasthan Single Sign On (SSO) scholarship. The selected students will get maximum rewards of up to INR 25,000 per annum.

Swami Vivekananda Scholarship

Eligiblity:Candidates studying class XI and above in West Bengal, India
Scholarship Amount:Up to INR 8,000
Last Date to apply:March 31, 2023

The state government of West Bengal organizes the Swami Vivekananda scholarship. This scholarship scheme is available for the students studying in class 11 and above. This scholarship for class 12 is to provide financial help to impoverished students and motivate them to pursue their higher education. Students who are inhabitants of West Bengal and those who belong to the economically weaker society are given monetary assistance by the government of West Bengal. The students pursuing MPhil, undergraduate, postgraduate, and Ph.D. degrees are eligible to apply for this program. The selected applicants will be rewarded up to INR 8,000.

College Board India Scholarship

Eligiblity:Students in class XI and class XII
Scholarship Amount:Up to 100% waiver on tuition fee
Last Date to apply:February 11, 2023

The College Board India scholarship aims to provide financial support to the students to pursue their studies in Indian universities to save their money by offering a discount while applying for the SAT exam. Under this program, the students should attend the Scholarship Aptitude Test (SAT) and score a minimum of 1350 out of the total 1600 score to be eligible for the scholarship. The program's sole aim is to provide scholarship opportunities to the students of class 11 and class 12. The selected students will get a 100% waiver on tuition fees here.

MYSY Scholarship

Eligiblity:Students who have passed the class X and class XII board exams from a recognised institution in Region Gujarat, India
Scholarship Amount:Up to INR 2,00,000
Last Date to apply:November 11, 2022

The state government of Gujarat organizes the Mukhyamantri Yuva Swavalamban Yojana (MYSY) scholarship. This scholarship is available for the students studying in classes 10 and 12 and is granted to those students who are domiciles of Gujarat. The primary aim of this scholarship for class 12 is to provide opportunities to economically weaker students studying in Gujarat. Students who have cleared their class 10th and 12th with a minimum mark of 80% are eligible to avail the benefits of this scholarship scheme. The selected students will get rewards up to INR 2,00,000 per annum.
