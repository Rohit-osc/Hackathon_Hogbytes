Reliance Foundation Scholarships

Eligiblity:Students must be enrolled in first-year full-time undergraduate degree in any stream from a recognised Indian institute. Must have passed class 12 with a minimum of 60% marks The annual household income should be less than INR 15,00,000 (preference will be given to those students whose family income is less than INR 2,50,000) Open for resident Indian citizens only.

Scholarship Amount:Up to INR 2,00,000 over the duration of the degree programme

Last Date to apply:14th February 2023

Reliance Foundation Scholarships are a pioneering initiative aimed at nurturing India’s technology leaders of tomorrow. Reliance Foundation will identify and support 100 of India’s brightest first-year undergraduate and postgraduate students who are pursuing degree programmes in Artificial Intelligence, Computer Sciences, Mathematics and Computing and Electrical and/or Electronics Engineering. Sitaram Jindal Foundation Scholarship

Eligiblity:Students pursuing class XI to postgraduate degree including ITI, diploma and professional courses

Scholarship Amount:Up to INR 3,200 per month

Last Date to apply:December 31, 2022

The Sitaram Jindal Foundation offers the Sitaram Jindal foundation scholarship for the students studying in classes 11-12/ ITI/ Diploma/ B.Tech/ bachelor's and master's courses. This scholarship scheme funds the education for the underprivileged candidates associated with the backward section of society. The meritorious students can avail a maximum B.Tech scholarship amount of INR 3,200 per month. The keen applicants must be below 30 years of age with an annual family earning that should not exceed INR 4,00,000.

Ratan Tata Scholarship for Engineering students

Eligiblity:Candidates with an admission offer to Cornell University's college of engineering

Scholarship Amount:Fee waivers

Last Date to apply:November 30, 2022

The Tata Education and Development Trust presents the Ratan Tata scholarship to engineering students willing to pursue their graduation at Cornell University. The scholarship provides funds and encourages academically exceptional students to study B.Tech and other courses abroad. To be eligible for the scholarship, they must be enrolled in a 4 years graduation program at Cornell University, New York. The deserving candidates will get a fee waiver for the tuition fees and other educational sessions. However, more preference will be given to the students enrolled in the College of Architecture.
